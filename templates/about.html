About KUHIN

Our Mission

To promote health informatics education and research at Kathmandu University, bridging the gap between healthcare and technology through innovation, collaboration, and practical application.

What is Health Informatics?

Health Informatics is the interdisciplinary field that uses information technology to organize and analyze health records to improve healthcare outcomes. It combines computer science, information science, and healthcare to improve the efficiency and quality of patient care.

At KUHIN, we focus on:

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) Systems
  • Health Data Analytics
  • Telemedicine Technologies
  • Healthcare Software Development
  • Medical Data Security

Club Objectives

  • Provide hands-on experience with health information systems
  • Organize workshops and seminars on health technology
  • Facilitate research in health informatics
  • Connect students with industry professionals
  • Participate in health tech competitions
  • Develop practical solutions for healthcare challenges

Who Can Join?

KUHIN welcomes students from all faculties who are interested in:

  • Computer Science & IT students interested in healthcare applications
  • Medical/Health sciences students interested in technology
  • Anyone passionate about improving healthcare through technology
  • Researchers looking for interdisciplinary collaboration
